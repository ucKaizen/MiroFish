## Action menu (what counts as "engagement")

The runner emits one of these per persona per round, gated by salience:

- `DO_NOTHING` — saw it, moved on.
- `LIKE_POST` — passive endorsement.
- `REPOST` — share to the persona's network.
- `CREATE_POST` — write their own take, in their voice.
- `REPLY` — engage in the comments under the launch post.

## Rules for prediction

- Strict raters stay strict, generous raters stay generous.
- Every prediction must be traceable to the panelist's recorded
  behaviour and voice examples.
- The 10 named panelists are the only participants — do not produce
  predictions for anyone else.
- Do not invent product features that are not in the overview.
- Distinguish between the product (MiroFish v2) and any example study
  it ships with (e.g. Sherwood S2). Reactions should be to the product.
