## Synopsis

Second run of James Graham's BBC One drama. Six episodes, around 60
minutes each, broadcast Sunday evenings on BBC One (and iPlayer in
full from launch night). Set in present-day ex-mining
Nottinghamshire, in the long shadow of the 1984-85 Miners' Strike —
a community where political loyalty, family allegiance, and economic
loss are still entangled. Series 2 picks up later in the same place:
organised crime, generational fracture, and local power running in
parallel and converging by the finale. Cast led by David Morrissey,
Lesley Manville, Monica Dolan, Robert Lindsay, and Stephen Dillane —
ensemble structure, no single protagonist. Tone: slow-burn,
politically engaged, demanding viewing.
